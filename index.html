Our Story

In the summer of 2023, our story began along the quiet, sun-soaked coast of Santa Barbara. Through God's grace, we found each other at just the right time. We instantly bonded through our nerdy interests of anime, fantasy worlds, theme parks, and classical music, as well as dreaming up the next place we could travel together. The joy of finding a partner with whom we could be completely ourselves was a true gift for us both.

Since then, we have filled our days with matching outfits, inside jokes, and the kind of quirky personalities our families lovingly remind us are very, very weird. We have learned that love can feel peaceful, playful, and wonderfully silly. We are incredibly excited to begin this next chapter hand in hand as husband and wife, outfit coordination almost certainly included.

Rules

A few kind requests

To help us keep the day peaceful, safe, and focused on the ceremony, we kindly ask all guests to abide by the following requests.

  • Invitation Only. To help us plan appropriately, please attend only if your name is included on the invitation.
  • Formal Attire Required. We kindly ask all guests to dress in formal attire for the celebration.
  • Silence phones during the ceremony. Please keep phones silenced so everyone can be fully present.
  • No smoking or vaping. Smoking and vaping are not permitted anywhere on venue property.
  • No ceremony photos. Please refrain from taking photos during the ceremony; our photographer will capture those moments for everyone.
